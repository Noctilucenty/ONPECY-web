Questions, answered.

Is ONPECY a course or an incubator?

An incubator. Lessons exist, but the goal is a working business system — AI agents, workflows, and a One Person Company model — not a certificate.

Do students need coding experience?

No. Students build with AI tools, automation platforms, and no-code/low-code workflows. The focus is operating systems, not writing everything by hand.

Is income guaranteed?

No. ONPECY teaches how revenue models work and how to build them. Actual income depends on execution, customer acquisition, market conditions, and business performance.

What platforms do students work with?

Workflows connected to ALLCPR, USJ, SVIHT, and SSI, using a stack that includes ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Cursor, automation tools, and CRM / knowledge-base systems.

Who reviews student work?

Work is built inside real workflows and reviewed against how the connected platforms actually operate, so feedback is practical rather than theoretical.

What happens after I apply?

We review your application and email qualified applicants with next steps: a short conversation, matching to a track and platform, and an invitation to build.
